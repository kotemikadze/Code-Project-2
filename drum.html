Snare

Snare drums come in all shapes and sizes. They are used in concert and orchestral music, marching band, pop music and many other genres.

The kick drum (also called the bass drum) isn’t the “showy” part of a drum kit, but it’s essential in creating the right sound for jazz, rock, and many other musical styles

Cymbals
